Youth Leadership Program

June 22, 2022

Design Photography Communnity

Our Youth Leadership Program official began in the summer of 2013. It started with a core group of 4 academically inclined students who were motivated to pay it forward. They wanted to mentor and tutor other youth members in our community. They passion to aid our youth and determintation to lead by example prompted me to support them and provide them with the necessary resources to succeed.

Since then we have had 100s of students be part of our program and succeed in their academic pursuits. Our original board for instance, all graduated on top of their class and attented prestigious universities such UCLA, Berkeley, and Pomona college. They paved the way for others like them to join and become part of something greater.

Mentorship

One of our core pillars of our program is our mentorship. Many of our fellows receive a mentor who helps teach them the ropes. They are there for them on one on one counseling and help them throughout their journey. Our mentors also tutors them academically. This is something we are deeply invested in since we want each of our fellows to pursue some form of higher academic learning.

Education is the passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today.

Malcom X

Education

We also believe in providing our fellows with a unique academic experience. One sector of our program is equiping our fellows with the necessary skills to be competitive individuals in our economy. As such, we take them to visit different industries that play a huge role in our economy. Some of the those places range from the Long Beach Port, to Terminal Island and its Federal Prison, to a manufacturing plant. We believe this form of exprience will not only expand their network but also help them undertand how interconnected these industries are and its importance in keeping our economy moving.

Through our network, we invite local, state and national leaders from diverse fields, including Fortune 500 companies, nonprofit organizations and government agencies, to expose our fellows to the interconnected network that bridge the public and private sector. Through the Youth Leadership Program, our fellows will gain insight to different industries, valuable experience and be inspired to reach their full potential as leaders in our communities.

The Youth Leadership Program will run for 8 months. During this time frame their will be one major industry we will visit per month. Each session will focus on a different issue area with students engaging with industry leaders and taking part in hands-on site visits. The program will commence in November and will culminate with a Graduation ceremony the folliwing summer.
