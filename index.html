Hello, we are Reynosos Hit N Walk. Helping the Southeast communities of Los Angeles since 2010.

Reynsos Hit N Walk was founded in 2010 with three goals in mind; serve our communities, mentor the youth, and support small businesses.

See some of our events or get in touch to discuss how we could help you.

We believe in the power of giving back to our communities. Join us as we continue to serve the communities that need it the most.

Services

Our list of awesome services.

We offer services many services to our communities, our youth, and small businesses that are partnered with us. We believe in helping others through our works and the events we host for community members at large.

Branding

One of the key features that enable us to help our small businesses is through branding. We help them by marketing many of the small businesses to the local communities through events we host. We also help them establish their presence online through their social media as well as their own website.

Youth Leadership Program

We believe in inspiring and mentoring the next group of leaders. At such, we have started a youth leadership program that instills the following core pillars; leadership, entreprenuership, education, and communication. We accomplish this through our field trips across various industries that make LA economy prosper.

Financial Consulting

Another service we provide to partnered business is financial consulting. We utilize our extensive network of people in the financial industry as well as the knowledge our board possesses to provide discounted services to small businesses.

Family Resources

We believe in the foundation of a strong family home. At such we provide them with services such as food, essentials, and even counseling. We believe that a strong family unit creates strong communities and a better future.

Community Events

We host a variety of events across the Southeast region of Los Angeles. Many of our events provide the commuities with resources and are driven on a charitable basis. We often collaborate with city councils in the Southeast region to bring awareness to all the resources offered to community members.

Drives

We host numerous dtives throughout the year which include food drives, toy drives, and numerous others that provide families with the essential goods to live a great lifestyle.

About Us

This is what we are.

Reynoso's Hit N' Walk focuses on facilitating healthy activities that promote physical fitness among citizens in the community. Reynoso's Hit N' Walk is a non-profit, tax-exempt organization devoted to promoting health among businesses and citizens. We work directly with communities and city councils to develop programs that promote three key pillars, community, healthy lifestyles, and entrepreneurship.
